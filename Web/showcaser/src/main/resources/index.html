Working with startups and entrepreneurs is a great experience. The one big challenge is how do I showcase my work without compromising on confidentiality and without revealing anything about the companies or ideas I work on?

Enter the NoFuss Showcase pages. Instead of showing you products I've worked on, I take small snippets from problems I've solved for various projects and present them in an anonymised fashion.

What this is not

Nothing here is a complete app nor is it styled for production. Instead, these are examples of things that can be achieved with maximum code-sharing between the mobile, web and server tiers. Think of it as a collage of snapshots over a period of several years.

Out of interest - almost all of the JavaScript used in this showcase has been built using Kotlin. You can find out more about Kotlin Multiplatform Projects here : Kotlin MPP.
